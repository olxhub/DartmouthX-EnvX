Food and Soils (Week 3) Learning Objectives:

  • Identify the major environmental issues related to global human nutrition
  • Describe some common practices of modern, industrial agriculture and the potential environmental costs associated with each practice
  • Identify and describe sustainable farming practices
  • Describe soil forming processes and common properties of soils
