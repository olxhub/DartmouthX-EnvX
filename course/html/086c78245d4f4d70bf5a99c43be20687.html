Global Change (Week 6) Learning Objectives:

  • Distinguish among global change, global climate change and global warming
  • Explain how solar radiation and greenhouse gases warm our planet
  • Discuss how CO2 concentrations and temperature on our planet have changed over time
  • Describe and identify feedback loops associated with global climate warming
  • Identify strategies for a more sustainable future
