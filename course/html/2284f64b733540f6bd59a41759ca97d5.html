Getting Help

There are several ways to interact with other students, course staff and edX Support.

To ask a question:

To ask a question about a video, reading or problem, click on Show Discussion immediately below the item and select Post Type: Question. Students in the course, community TAs and/or instructors will respond. 

To discuss:

To disucss a video, reading or problem, click on Show Discussion immediately below the item and select Post Type: Question. Type: Discussion.

To request technical help:

If you need technical help on how to use the edX platform, post a question in Questions About the Course (click Show Discussion below). Another student, community TAs or course staff will respond as soon as possible. 

To report a problem to edX, click on the 'Help' tab, located on the left side of your screen. Note that edX Student Help will usually respond by email to Report a Problem tickets within 24 hours. 

Click Pre-questionnaire on the left menu to continue.
