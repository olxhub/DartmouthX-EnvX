The Human Population (Week 2) Learning Objectives:

  • Describe global population trends 
  • Understand age-structure diagrams and use the diagrams to make predictions about future population dynamics
  • Describe the differences in population changes and age structures between developing and developed countries
  • Explain how human population and development may cause environmental harm
