Prompted Discussion

In previous Prompted Discussions you wrote about your personal environmental impact, including Week 3, Your Favorite Meal and in Weeks 4 and 5, Energy That Flows Through Your Fingertips.

This week we ask, what’s missing? What other areas of your life would you evaluate from an environmental impact perspective?

You could consider your use of another energy supply, or water, or land or many other resources.

Prompted Discussion Assignment

Write a post that presents one other area that you wish to evaluate. Include the following information:

      • The country you live in (and if it is a large country, what region).
      • Which resource did you choose to evaluate? 
      • What measures and units would you use to assess this resource use and your resulting impact?
      • Consider posting a figure or diagram that illustrates your evaluation.

Finally, ask an environmental science question related to someone else's post about their evaluation.
