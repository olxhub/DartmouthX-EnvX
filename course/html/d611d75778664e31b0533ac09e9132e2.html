An unprompted discussion is a forum to ask a specific question or discuss points from the preceding material. We encourage you to discuss concepts, ask questions and help out other learners. These unprompted discussions are optional.

A prompted discussion will appear once each week where we ask you to respond to a specific query. You will receive credit for participating in these discussions. 

Tips for Success

Participate: You will get out what you put in, so be active.

Search: Before posting, search the Discussion for similar ideas. Your contribution will be most valuable if you add to an existing thread by replying and/or clicking on the green plus button to upvote the post.

Feedback: If you disagree with a post, respond using evidence and reasoning obtained from this course or reputable sources. 

Upvote: If you find a post by someone else to be especially helpful, "Upvote" it by clicking on the green plus (+) sign. 

Dashboard: Use the Discussion Dashboard to see new activity, posts you are following, and check replies to your posts. Use the filter settings and search in the left post stream to customize your view.
