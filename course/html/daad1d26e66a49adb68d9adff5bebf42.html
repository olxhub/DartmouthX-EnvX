Discussion Prompt: Age-structure diagram

Responding to the following discussion prompt is worth one point.

Now that you understand what an age-structure diagram is, we would like you to create one of your own. The US Census Bureau allows you to generate a population pyramid for a particular country, in a particular year. 

Building an age-structure diagram: 

1) Identify a country, your own or one you are interested in.
2) Click on this link 
3) Choose Population Pyramid Graph under Select Report
4) Select 2015 under Select up to 25 Years
5) Select your country of interest (from step 1) in Custom Region
6) click submit button on the lower left of the page

Repeat these steps using 2050 in step 4. 

Submit a post listing the name of the country and the changes that occur during the 35 year period. If you can, include an image of the age-structure diagrams or the URL.
