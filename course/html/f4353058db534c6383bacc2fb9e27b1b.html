In this course, we will begin each week by introducing the learning objectives and topics covered. Here we present the learning objectives for Week 1.

Intro to Environmental Science (Week 1) Learning Objectives:

  • Define and understand the importance of environmental science and how it differs from environmental studies and environmentalism
  • Understand the three different levels of biodiversity (genetic, species, ecosystem)
  • Describe the carbon, nitrogen and hydrologic cycles
  • Know the components and states of matter and energy
  • Understand how inputs, outputs, steady states and feedbacks may affect environmental systems
